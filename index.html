Here's a time line of Playboi Carti's life:

  • 1996 - Born in Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
  • 2013 - Begins his music career, recording his first tracks and uploading them to SoundCloud under the pseudonym Sir Cartier.
  • 2015 - Changes his stage name to Playboi Carti. In the same year, he collaborates with Father and Awful Records, gaining initial recognition in the underground rap scene.
  • 2016 - Moves to New York City to focus on music. Joins the A$AP Mob collective and signs with A$AP Rocky's label, AWGE.
  • 2017 - Releases his debut self-titled mixtape, *Playboi Carti*. The mixtape includes hits like "Magnolia" and "Wokeuplikethis", which propel him to mainstream success.
  • 2018 - Drops his debut studio album, "Die Lit", which debuts at number three on the Billboard 200. The album receives critical acclaim and solidifies his position in the rap industry.
  • 2019 - Teases his highly anticipated second studio album, "Whole Lotta Red". His fanbase grows significantly due to his enigmatic style and unique sound.
  • 2020 - Releases Whole Lotta Red on Christmas Day, which debuts at number one on the Billboard 200. The album polarizes listeners but cements Carti's status as a pioneer of experimental rap.
  • 2021 - Embarks on the "King Vamp" tour, performing tracks from "Whole Lotta Red". His gothic aesthetic becomes a major part of his brand.
  • 2022 - Begins teasing new music and collaborations, continuing to influence the rap and fashion worlds.
  • 2024 - Releases a few new tracks, including ALL RED, FIELD TRIP, Carnival, Type Shit. Most of his new album tracks are leaked. The AI song with his voice "TELESCOPE" becames a big viral on the internet
